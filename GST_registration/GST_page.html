GST Registration

India's Goods & Services Tax (GST) has boosted and diversified the economy in numerous key ways. This official policy covers all Indian service providers, which includes freelancers, manufacturers, and traders. GST is a neat amalgamation of sundry central taxes – service, excise duty, CST – and various state taxes – entertainment, luxury, octroi, VAT. GST registration can readily be done online. There is little need for in-person involvement in this regard. Taxpayers with a turnover of less than Rs.1.5 crore have the option of choosing a composition scheme. This can help rid them of GST formalities and pay it at a fixed turnover rate. We rocmatters help guide you on all such matters pertaining to GST registration.

Documents Required

  • PAN of the GST Registration Applicant
  • Proof of Business Registraion or Incorporation Certificate
  • Identity & Address Proof of Promoters with Photographsl
  • Address Proof for the place of Business
  • Bank Account Statement Showing Name, Address & a Few Transactions
  • Class 2 Digital Signature For the Authorised Signatory
